Ars Electronica performance

With Peter Herbert (AT) on Double Bass and Hannes Löschel (AT) on piano

This performance was by Ars Sonorum which is part of Ars Electronica The performance was in the Sonic Lab at the Anton Brucker University in Linz, using their fantastic multichannel audio system. The performance took place on September 8th, 2018.

Raflost performance

With Eirikur Orri Olafsson on the trumpet. May 22nd, 2020

The Raflost festival took place during Covid-19 in Reykjavik. No audience, except in a nearby shop/bar called 12 Tonar.

Helsinki Performance

With Tommi Keränen on electric guitar, November 15th, 2019

A free improvisation with noise artist Tommi Keränen at the Kallio Stage in Helsinki. This was part of a symposium on new musical instruments organised by Koray Tarhiglu, and the concert included some fantastic performances, for example by Jukka Kääriäinen on guitar.

Brain Dead Ensemble in the Green Door Store

For Process Disperson performance in Brighton, September 6th, 2018

The Brain Dead Ensemble (Alice Eldridge - feedback cello, Chris Kiefer - feedback cello, Thanos Polymeneas-Liontiris - feedback bass, Thor Magnusson - threnoscope) if formed of experimental musicians working at the Emute Lab at the University of Sussex.

Open Sound Collective gig at the Verdict

with Alice Eldridge (Cello) and Thanos Polymeneas Liontiris (double bass)

This performance was a predecessor of the Brain Dead Ensemble, before Halldor Ulfarsson brought his halldorophone to Sussex, resulting in feedbac cellos and double basses
